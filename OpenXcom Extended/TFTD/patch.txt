OpenXcom Universal TFTD Patch
==============================

This patch covers all community fixes to the original X-COM: Terror From The Deep data that affect OpenXcom.

To install, extract to your OpenXcom\TFTD folder, overwriting every file.

If you need to patch the original game instead, you should check here: http://www.strategycore.co.uk/files/x-com-terror-from-the-deep/patches/
